Seng Bum Michael Yoo

Michael is interested in understanding the neural population inferring the world when a biological agent (either animal or human) is foraging/hunting in interactive and dynamic environments. Michael got his B.S in biomedical science and psychology at Handong University. He obtained his Ph.D. in neuroscience from the University of Minnesota (Advisor: Dr. Benjamin Hayden) and subsequently did his postdoc at the Massachusetts Institution of Technology (Advisor: Dr. Mehrdad Jazayeri).
